# User Guide

## Getting Started

Welcome to our AI-powered journaling platform. This guide will help you make the most of our features.

### Setting Up Your Account

1. **Sign Up**
   - Visit the signup page
   - Choose your preferred authentication method (LINE, Kokoa, Instagram, or Twitter)
   - Complete your profile

2. **Writing Your First Journal Entry**
   - Click the "New Entry" button
   - Write freely about your thoughts and feelings
   - Use prompts if you need inspiration
   - Save your entry

### Key Features

#### AI Analysis
Our AI analyzes your entries to help you understand:
- Emotional patterns
- Recurring themes
- Personal growth opportunities

#### Privacy & Security
- All entries are encrypted
- You control who can see your entries
- Two-factor authentication available

### Tips for Better Journaling

1. **Be Consistent**
   - Set a regular time for journaling
   - Use reminders if helpful
   - Don't worry about perfect writing

2. **Use AI Insights**
   - Review your emotional trends
   - Explore suggested themes
   - Consider growth recommendations

### Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues:
1. Check our FAQ section
2. Contact support through the help center
3. Use the feedback form for suggestions
